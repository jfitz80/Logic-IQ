Logic IQ Privacy Policy Effective Date: June 1, 2026 Logic IQ is a reasoning puzzle game built around deduction, rules, order puzzles, and grid-based logic challenges. We do not collect, sell, or share personal information. Logic IQ may store game progress locally on your device, including score, streaks, badges, difficulty progress, daily challenge status, and app preferences. This information stays on your device and is used only to support gameplay. If you enable daily reminder notifications, Logic IQ uses your device’s local notification system to send reminders. Notification preferences are managed on your device and can be changed in iOS Settings. Logic IQ does not use third-party advertising trackers, does not sell user data, and does not require account registration.
